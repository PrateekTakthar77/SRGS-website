About SRGS

Shri Ram Global School, Karnal started in the year 20 is a CBSE-affiliated co-ed school with a campus of acres.

The Shri Ram Family is known for its commitment to quality education and has various schools and institutions under its umbrella. The Shri Ram Schools are renowned for providing a holistic education and emphasizing values, character development, and academic excellence. Some well-known institutions are Shri Ram College of Commerce and Lady Shri Ram College at Delhi University.

Vision
